The Preserve Tibet Project

Development Stage: Fund raising
We hope to complete the project by July 2013

Mission

Our plan is to find, collect and digitize at-risk collections both private and public, using standard tools and methods of digital preservation and best practices so that these treasures can be saved before they fade into oblivion.


Project K
We are starting the project with a particular archive that we've code named "K". As interesting as the story of the archive is, we cannot reveal the details in public because of certain complications, hence the code name. We will share updates on the project on this page as it progresses.

The archive has an extraordinary collection of teachings from the greatest masters of our time, many of whom have passed away. The archive is poorly maintained, stored at facility with no temperature or humid control of any kind and hence many precious recordings have already been damaged.

Among others, the archive contains forgotten but precious recordings of Khunu Lama Tenzin Gyaltsen, Kyabje Dudjom Rinpoche, Kyabje Chatral Rinpoche and Jamyang Khyentse Chokyi Lodro.
